SMART WEARABLE TECH

Smart Technology

Focuing in on AR smart glasses enhance real-world experiences by overlaying digital information using transparent displays, sensors, and AI-driven features. They enable hands-free interaction through voice commands, gesture recognition, and wireless connectivity. Common applications include workplace efficiency in manufacturing and healthcare, real-time navigation, immersive gaming, interactive education, and AR-assisted retail experiences. However, challenges such as battery life, limited field of view, high costs, and privacy concerns remain. Future advancements focus on AI integration, lighter designs, improved battery efficiency, wider displays, and 5G/cloud connectivity, making AR smart glasses more practical and accessible.
