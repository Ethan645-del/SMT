DISPLAY TECH

Smart AR glasses with advanced display technologies like micro-LED, OLED, and high-resolution displays offer greater images but consume more power, which shortens the battery's lifespan. Higher brightness, refresh rates, and resolutions need more energy, especially in compact devices with limited batteries. Rich contrasts and vibrant colors are provided by micro-LED panels, but they also use more power, particularly in bright environments. In response, manufacturers are developing dynamic refresh rates, power-saving modes, and energy-efficient display technologies to reconcile battery life with visual performance. For smart glasses, however, striking this balance remains a significant design problem.
