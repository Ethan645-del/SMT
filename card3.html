Connectivity Features

Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and cellular networks all require continuous communication between devices, which increases energy consumption. Smart device network modules, such as AR glasses, must be powered continuously to enable remote interactions, cloud synchronization, and real-time data transfers. Complex connections (like 5G or high-speed Wi-Fi) require more energy to function. The continuing need for power from connection services may accelerate battery depletion, especially in portable devices with limited battery capacity. By using energy-efficient wireless technologies and improving connection management, performance and battery life may be matched.
