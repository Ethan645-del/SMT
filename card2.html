Processing Power

Advanced features like artificial intelligence (AI), real-time language translation, and augmented reality demand a lot of processing power to handle complex tasks like machine learning, data processing, and real-time rendering of AR content. This significantly raises energy consumption. Consequently, the energy requirement increases with feature sophistication, placing strain on battery life, particularly in portable devices with constrained power sources. High-demand features still cause quicker battery depletion, despite manufacturers focusing on more energy-efficient CPUs and software optimization to balance performance with battery use.
